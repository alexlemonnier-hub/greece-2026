04

Athens — the city days

Three nights, all on foot from the hotel. One big ancient-sites day, framed by an easy arrival evening and a gentler bonus day.

Friday 29 May

Arrive & settle evening

Land 15:45, your pre-paid taxi straight to the hotel (~40 min) — driver waiting at arrivals; settle by ~17:00. A wander through Plaka, then the Lycabettus funicular (no climbing) for the sunset (~20:40).

Dinner: Avocado or Vegan Beat → see Eat

Saturday 30 May

The big ancient-Athens day

Start cool at the Ancient Agora (~09:15), then your booked 12:00 slot at the Acropolis & Parthenon — North-Slope lift, hats & water (no shade at midday). Lunch, then the air-conditioned Acropolis Museum (booked 15:00) through the heat, and the Temple of Olympian Zeus at dusk.

Dinner: ★ Makris ✅ 19:30 — rooftop, Parthenon view → see Eat

Sunday 31 May

The bonus day — gentler

Morning at the National Archaeological Museum (world-class, A/C, accessible). Afternoon, your pick: the Panathenaic Stadium + National Garden (flat & central) or a Cape Sounion sunset.

Dinner: Veganaki or Cookoomela → see Eat

05

Kefalonia — the island days

Four nights from a Pesada base: a half-day arrival, two exploring days, and a relaxed finale. Relax + some exploring, all by car.

Monday 1 June

Transfer & settle half day

Checkout by 11:00, fly ATH→KEF (13:25–14:25), pick up the car, ~15 min to Pesada. Gentle first evening at a nearby southern beach — Avithos or Ai Helis.

Tuesday 2 June

The North loop — the scenic big day

Leave ~8:30: Myrtos viewpoint & beach early, then Assos (flat & lovely — skip the castle climb) and a long lunch in Fiskardo.

~1 h each way — the one long drive, and worth it.

Wednesday 3 June

Caves & the east coast

To Sami (~40 min): Melissani Cave by boat (best light 11:30–14:00), optional Drogarati, then a swim at Antisamos.

Thursday 4 June

Argostoli, the farm lunch & relax

Flat morning in Argostoli, lunch at En Kefallinia farm (~7 min), a Lassi beach afternoon, and turtles at dusk in the Koutavos lagoon.

Friday 5 June

Home — early

Leave Pesada ~08:00, car back by 08:30, fly EFL→ZRH 10:35–14:20 via Vienna. Pack the night before.

Janine's allergy card

Vegetarian (no meat/fish/seafood) + a medical allergy to the onion family (onion, shallot, leek, spring onion, chives). Garlic in small amounts is fine. Print a few copies; show it to every kitchen.

Σοβαρή αλλεργία & διατροφικές ανάγκες

Είμαι χορτοφάγος. Δεν τρώω κρέας, πουλερικά, ψάρι ή θαλασσινά.

Έχω σοβαρή αλλεργία στην οικογένεια του κρεμμυδιού: κρεμμύδι, ασκαλώνια, πράσο, φρέσκο κρεμμυδάκι, σχοινόπρασο, καθώς και σκόνη κρεμμυδιού και ζωμούς/σάλτσες που τα περιέχουν. Αυτά πρέπει να αποφεύγονται εντελώς.

Το σκόρδο σε μικρή ποσότητα είναι εντάξει — δεν χρειάζεται να το αφαιρέσετε, αλλά με μέτρο.

Η αλλεργία είναι ιατρική, όχι προτίμηση. Παρακαλώ ετοιμάστε το φαγητό μου χωρίς κρεμμύδι και τα συγγενικά του, με καθαρά σκεύη. Ευχαριστώ πολύ!

English: Vegetarian (no meat/poultry/fish/seafood) + a medical allergy to the onion family — onion, shallot, leek, spring onion, chives (incl. onion powder & stocks), avoid entirely. Garlic in small amounts is fine. Cook with no onion or its relatives, clean utensils. Tip: have a Greek speaker double-check the wording.
